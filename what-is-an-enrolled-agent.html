What Is an Enrolled Agent?

A practical explanation of the Enrolled Agent credential and IRS representation rights.

By Chris Dykstra, EA6 min readLast reviewed July 2026
  • The credential is issued at the federal level and is focused on taxation.
  • Enrolled Agents may represent individuals and businesses before the IRS, subject to federal rules.
  • Unlimited practice rights are not restricted by taxpayer type or geography.
  • An EA may be a strong fit for planning, preparation, notices, audits, collections, and appeals.
Key takeaway

Choose a tax professional based on the work you need, the person's experience, and the quality of communication.
