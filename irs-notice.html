IRS Notices

What to do when a letter from the IRS arrives

An IRS notice can be stressful, but the best response is usually calm and methodical.

Read the entire notice

Identify the tax year, issue, amount, deadline, and response instructions.

Confirm the notice is legitimate

Compare the contact information and account details with official records. Be cautious of unexpected demands for unusual payment methods.

Compare it with your return and records

Gather the filed return, supporting documents, payment confirmations, and prior correspondence.

Respond by the deadline

Even when you disagree, do not ignore the notice. A timely, documented response preserves options and helps prevent escalation.
