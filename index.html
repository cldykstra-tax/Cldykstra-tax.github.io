Why Work With an Enrolled Agent?

Federal tax expertise with the authority to represent taxpayers.

Enrolled Agents are licensed by the federal government and specialize in taxation. They can represent taxpayers before the IRS in matters involving examinations, collections, and appeals.

01

Unlimited Practice Rights

Representation rights are not limited by taxpayer type or geographic location.

02

Tax-Focused Education

Continuing education requirements focus on federal taxation and professional ethics.

03

Year-Round Support

Tax planning and representation extend well beyond filing season.
